Grammy Awards

The first Grammy Awards ceremony was held May 4th, 1959 to honor and respect the musical accomplishments. The awards are separated into categories, a general field and by genre. In 2012 the amount of categories went from 109 to 78. After nominees occur, final voting ballets are sent to members. Voting occurs based on quality alone, and cannot be influenced by sales, chart performance, loyalty or bias. However, there have been criticism in the awards and how it is determined. The Grammy Awards are currently telecasted. Many years, the day is the second Sunday of February.

Quote

“A GRAMMY is awarded by The Recording Academy's voting membership to honor excellence in the recording arts and sciences. It is truly a peer honor, awarded by and to artists and technical professionals for artistic or technical achievement, not sales or chart positions. -Source| Grammy.com
